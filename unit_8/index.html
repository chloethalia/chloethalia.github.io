OUR STORY

Chloe's is a small pastry shop located in the idyllic, but non existant town of West New Loughton, NY. Founded in 2004 with just a few basic recipes; Chloe's has expanded its selection to nearly two hundred different pastries that cycle with the months. Chloe's is a can't miss stop on West New Loughton's famous Main Street.
