LOCATION

Chloe's is located on the corner of Main street and Gwendolyn drive. It's directly across from John Jay's Hardware and right next door to Ada's Wedding Dress Boutique. If you get lost you can always ask one of our many friendly residents; they'll be glad to point you in the right direction. Then just look for the red and white awning and you've arrived. Be sure to visit many of the other shops just a step away on this historic street.
